Sample fridge

The fridge is where you'll find all your products listed with their days until expiry.

A few key features:

  • Insert products
  • Delete products
  • Search fridge
  • Sort fridge by each column

Fill your fridge!

When the add product button is clicked from the fridge view, the user is redirected to the add product page.

The user can then decide to add a new product or choose from previously submitted products.

Mobile Friendly

FridgeMate was originally intended to be a mobile application, therefore it's completely responsive to smaller screens.

Email Notifications

Choose when and how often you'd like to receive updates on your fridge.
